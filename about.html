ABOUT US

We here at Web Based Games create an environment for fellow gamers to enjoy and compete with themselves. It all started with a dream to create a common platform for all those who try to test their abilities and enhance them further. We believe that to bring out the best version of oneself, one need to compete with himself, that too in a way that you keep enjoying yourself.As to make the fact reality of everyone enjoying and having pleasure in their own time regardless of any interruptions caused to them of any type , we created this platform. We here believe in that and keeps moving forward in order to achieve that.

The main intention behind building this system is that we want every person, no matter what OS they run on their computer, to be able to enjoy some cross platform game.
